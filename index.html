How it works

  1. Enter your name exactly as it appears on your birth certificate — first, middle, and last. The letters power the numerology.
  2. Enter your birth date — required by every system.
  3. Add your birth time and city if you can. These are optional, but they unlock your rising sign and the full imperial Zi Wei chart. The time zone and coordinates are worked out automatically from the city.
  4. Cast the chart — and read all nine traditions side by side, each term explained on hover.

These traditions are offered for reflection, insight, and delight — not as science or certainty. The numerology follows the esoteric method set out in Shirley Blackwell Lawrence's The Secret Science of Numerology, presented in the honest historical light of Andrew Gregory's scholarship on the actual Pythagoreans, who did not practise name-numerology as we know it today.
